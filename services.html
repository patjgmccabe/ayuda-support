Support Broker Services

Professional support tailored to your needs.

What We Do

As a Support Broker, we work directly with individuals and families enrolled in OPWDD self-directed services. Our role is to guide you through every step — from understanding your options to managing your budget and hiring staff.

  • Help with developing and managing your self-directed budget
  • Assistance in hiring, training, and supervising support staff
  • Guidance through the Fiscal Intermediary process
  • Support with Circle of Support meetings and planning
  • Advocacy and coordination with service providers
  • Ongoing communication and progress tracking

We are committed to empowering you with the knowledge and resources you need to live a fulfilling, self-directed life.
